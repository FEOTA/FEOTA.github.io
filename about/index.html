FEOTA

Federation of Experts on Theoretical & Applied Sciences

Who we are

Federation of Experts On Theoretical & Applied Sciences, also known as FEOTA, is a joint effort run by a dedicated team of young scientists based in Finland. The company officially launched its operations on 2 August 2021 and has its office in Espoo, Finland.

FEOTA started its journey as an unregistered non-profit organization in 2011 by offering affordable virtual introductory courses on radio astronomy to students around the world. The high demand for science-based courses and a large number of inquiries regarding career opportunities in STEM led us to rethink the scale of our operation. Consequently, we extended our services to consultancy, science and language tutoring as well as science content creation after years of careful planning. Today, FEOTA Oy stands to connect the young and aspiring scientists with the curious-minded youth in hopes of turning many dreams into reality.

Vision

FEOTA aims to popularize science and scientific thinking among youth around the world. We envision a future where rationality and scientific reasoning will prevail over dogma, pseudoscience and superstitions in the minds of our youth.

Mission

The mission of FEOTA is to provide quality services based on four key principles: honesty, transparency, reliability and affordability. In light of the ongoing scientific advancement around the globe, we want to help promising minds find their way into STEM research and contribute to this progress.
