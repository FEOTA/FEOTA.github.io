What We Offer

FEOTA offers a range of services to students, universities and job seekers, respectively. We help students get into their dream universities in Europe to study STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics).

Simultaneously, we promote European universities to help attract foreign talents towards their international degree programmes. We provide affordable consulting service to job seekers looking for career opportunities in the STEM fields in Europe.

We also offer high quality physics, chemistry, mathematics, IELTS, TOEFL and GRE tutoring services to students and university applicants.

Who We Are

We are a multidisciplinary and multicultural team of early-stage researchers and scientists with a shared vision of supporting young science enthusiasts around the world. Our team members come from 5 different countries and speak 6 different languages; however, the passion for science unites us all in an effort to understand the nature around us and share our knowledge with others.
