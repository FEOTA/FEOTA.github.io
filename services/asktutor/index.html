AskTutor

You aim, we help!

AskTutor is an unique service we developed, keeping students from diverse background and their individual needs in mind.

Through this service we offer high quality physics, chemistry, mathematics, biology and English language tuition at an affordable price. You have the freedom to choose any number of sessions for the subjects mentioned in the price chart below. If you are looking for regular tuition, pick one of our packages to save a buck. It is possible to arrange the sessions either online (for students living outside Helsinki region) or in-person (for students living within Helsinki region) depending on the availability, area of expertise and location of the tutors.

The registration process is free of charge, easy and quick

  • Fill up the contact form with your name, email and country of residence.
  • Choose ‘AskTutor science’, if you need help with physics, chemistry, mathematics and/or biology.
  • Choose ‘AskTutor language’, if you need help with English language skills.
  • Check the price chart below for service fees.
  • In the message box, write us a short text about your educational background, requirements and service you want to buy.
  • Submit and wait for our response.

Our representative will contact you by email and guide you through the payment process. Once the payment is made, you will be connected to your tutor and the sessions will start as agreed.
