Consultancy

Our consultants are alumni from some of the world's top ranking universities!

Education consultancy is one of our key services. We carefully developed the service packages to help enthusiastic science students realize their careers in the field of STEM.

To be successful in launching a career in STEM, the importance of studying at a reputable university is paramount. Other than the ranking of the university, research profile of the lecturers, quality of scientific publications and industry connections are the vital factors one must take into account before shortlising the target universities. It is also important to plan in advance as each application takes much time, money and effort.

Our expert consultants are themselves graduates from the top ranking universities in Finland; a country that is world-renowened for its education system. Therefore, not only that you get step-by-step guidance to properly arrange admission and visa related papers but also valuable tips to improve your motivation letter and an opportunity to learn more about the country you are willing to visit for higher education.

If you are confident with your English languauge skills but looking for some extra help, try our basic package. The silver package is meant for those who would like to brush up their English language skills in addition to the admission and visa interview preparations. However, if you require extensive help then go for the gold package.

None of these packages suit you? No worries, get in touch with us and we will work out a custom package for you!
