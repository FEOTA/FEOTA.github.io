Terms of Use

FEOTA AskTutor
Last update: Feb 28, 2022

 

Every Customer gets a 25 minutes-long introductory session free of charge as a courtesy of their first time purchase of Our tutoring services. The introductory session is always arranged online between the Tutor and the Customer.

 

Face-to-face tuition

  • The price of a one-hour session includes the base hourly fee and compensation for the Tutor’s trip for up to 10 km from his/her place of residence to the agreed location.
  • Compensation for any distance exceeding 10 km is charged on a per-kilometer basis as 0.62€/km.
  • The confirmed session(s) can only be canceled 24 h prior to the meeting time without any cancellation fee. Otherwise, if the session is canceled minimum 8 h before the meeting time then the Customer has to pay a cancellation fee equivalent to half of the base hourly fee. Any cancellation beyond the 8 h limit will result in a cancellation fee equivalent to the full base hourly fee.
  • The confirmed session(s) can only be rescheduled 24 h prior to the meeting time without any service fee. Otherwise, if the session is rescheduled minimum 2 h before the meeting time then the Customer has to pay a service fee of 10€. Any rescheduling beyond the 2 h limit will result in a service fee equivalent to the full base hourly fee.
  • If the Customer fails to show up in a confirmed session without informing Us in advance by writing, a full price of the session will be charged as penalty fee.

 

Online tuition

  • The price of a one-hour session includes the base hourly fee.
  • The confirmed session(s) can only be canceled 24 h prior to the meeting time without any cancellation fee. Otherwise, if the session is canceled minimum 8 h before the meeting time then the Customer has to pay a cancellation fee equivalent to half of the base hourly fee. Any cancellation beyond the 8 h limit will result in a cancellation fee equivalent to the full base hourly fee.
  • The confirmed session(s) can only be rescheduled 24 h prior to the meeting time without any service fee. Any rescheduling after this time limit will result in a service fee of 10€.
  • If the Customer fails to show up in a confirmed session without informing Us in advance by writing, a full price of the session will be charged as penalty fee.

Every paying Customer receives a one-time 10% discount on the monthly total price for submitting us a review or feedback online (conditions apply).

 

– The discount must be used within two months from the start date.
– The discount cannot be used by Customers whom We agreed to provide services at a reduced price on humanitarian grounds.
